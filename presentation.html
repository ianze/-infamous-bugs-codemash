AT&T Long Distance

9 hours engineers working with law enforcement

Call Waiting...

Failure at one switch
Another switch was reset for new configuration.
Messages sent too fast

The Results

$60M in long distance calls
9 hours outage time
75M phone calls
200,000 airline reservations
Brand/PR damage

Why This Bug

Improving component performance can lead to adverse effect
Consider each change on the whole system

Patriot System Failure

Patriot used since the 60s. 90s first time against scud misses.
On February 25th 1991 a scud missile that has been fired at a US Air base in dhahrhan.
The system detected the missles but there was no intercept

Failure At Dahhran

Converting time to 24 bit integer. Lost precesion. Only one method protected
Drift caused missle not to be detected
More time the system is in operation the bigger the drift.
This system 100 hours, 8 hours - 20%. 20 hours - unusable

The Results

28 killed
110 injured
Mainly reserve duty personal from Pennsylvania.

Why This Bug

The issue reported but not prioritized
A gap between how the system is used and how it was assumed to be used
A fix has shipped and was delivered to the base the next day
